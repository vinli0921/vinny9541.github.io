My Experiences

RECYCLIFY

At BostonHacks, I co-developed a web app called Recyclify using the Keras library in Python to classify recyclability of waste. Trained on a dataset of 15k images, our CNN model achieved 80% accuracy, improving waste sorting efficiency and promoting sustainability. Presented at BostonHacks, showcasing our commitment to tech-driven social impact.

LEVOX

At Berkeley, I led a team of 3 developers to create speech-based English to foreign language translation software called LeVox for the CalHacks AI hackathon. Using WhisperAI, Google Cloud TTS Library, and DeepL API, we produced a prototype with an 85% projected accuracy across multiple languages.

BUCG

At Boston University Consulting Group, I led a team of 5 in the Strategy Lab competition. We developed a Venmo credit card adoption program, leveraging Qualtrics survey data to craft compelling visuals for the final pitch deck using Google Slides. Selected from 100+ applicants, our team placed in the top 4 among 19 teams, showcasing our ability to facilitate collaboration and translate consumer preferences into actionable strategies.

BUFC

At Boston University Finance & Investment Club, I served as a Junior Quantitative Analyst. I employed Geometric Brownian motions and Monte Carlo methods to simulate projected stock and index fund prices, achieving an average accuracy of 80%. Additionally, I participated in 5 projects to develop proficiency in Git and Python libraries such as NumPy, pandas, Matplotlib, and PyTorch, strengthening my analytical skills and technical expertise.
